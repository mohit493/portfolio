Hello Spotify, I am Mohit.

  • Paid music subscription survey
View the survey

I have been a Spotify premium user for over a year. I really like Spotify’s catalog of pre-created playlists and the recommendations available based on moods. However, In the past year, I have seriously considered canceling my premium account twice.

In my literature exploration, I found out research investigating user's attitude towards paying for streaming services (Link to some literature). However, I could not find good research investigating users behaviors leading to un-subscribing their paid music service.

After a session of self-contemplation I realized my main reason to consider unsubscribing; is the feeling of lack of familiarness to my music. Earlier as an iTunes user, I was curating my own music environment hence feeling in control and aware of the music I was listening too. However, in Spotify, I am listening to music in sync with my mood but not completely familiar with the music. Hence, I designed a survey to investigate the space of user unsubscription.

The survey method is chosen becasue in my given context of limited time and resources; it provides an effective way to sample users (posting in Reddit community or sharing on social media) and quantify results.

Possible impacts from the research insights

  • Alteration of user onboarding process.
  • Alteration of the user journey of curating one’s music collection.
  • Alteration of the user subscription flow.
  • Alteration of music recommendation algorithm.
  • Alteration in the product promotion strategy.
View the survey

The survey is designed in an informal explorative manner. The aim is not to find significant correlation but to explore attitudes and then be able to design more in depth research exploration.

PS: the survey was created using a free service hence was not able to add advanced features like logic partition to the survey flow.
